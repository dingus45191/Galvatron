A champion gladiator, D-16 who named himself after "Megatronus Prime"- one of the orginal 13 primes who betrayed his brothers and became the first decepticon. Megatron vowed to bring down the Caste system and bring equality for the masses. This reveloutionary started the 'Decipticon Movement' which would end up waging a war which destroyed Cybertron.
